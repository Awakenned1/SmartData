Our Services

Comprehensive Smart Solutions

At SmartData Traffic Solutions, we offer a wide range of professional services — from traffic engineering and data analytics to custom software development. Our team ensures speed, accuracy, and professionalism in every project.

01

Traffic Data Collection

Advanced traffic data collection using embedded sensors, pneumatic tubes, video detection systems, and real-time monitoring equipment for accurate traffic flow analysis.

  • Vehicle counting & classification
  • Speed measurement & analysis
  • Volume studies & peak hour data
  • Real-time traffic monitoring
02

Traffic Engineering

Professional traffic engineering solutions including signal optimization, intersection design, traffic flow management, and comprehensive safety assessments for improved road efficiency.

  • Signal timing optimization
  • Intersection design & analysis
  • Traffic flow management
  • Road safety assessments
03

Data Analytics

Transform raw data into actionable insights with our advanced analytics services. We provide comprehensive data analysis, visualization, predictive modeling, and business intelligence solutions tailored to your needs.

  • Data mining & pattern recognition
  • Predictive modeling & forecasting
  • Interactive dashboards & visualization
  • Business intelligence & reporting
04

Software Development

Custom software solutions designed to streamline your operations and enhance productivity. From web applications to mobile apps and enterprise systems, we build scalable, secure, and user-friendly solutions.

  • Custom web & mobile applications
  • API development & integration
  • Database design & management
  • Cloud solutions & deployment
05

Transportation Planning

Strategic transportation planning services to optimize mobility networks and infrastructure. We provide comprehensive feasibility studies, network analysis, and long-term strategic planning solutions.

  • Infrastructure development support

Project Management
